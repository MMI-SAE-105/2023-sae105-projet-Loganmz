Suicide Squad Volume 1

ONE Exciting Book

Suicide Squad: Volume 1 - Kicked in the Teeth is a comic book. A team of super-villains is recruited by the U.S. government to prevent a group of terrorists from using a nuclear bomb. The story is full of twists and suspense.

An explosive and dark start for the new team

DC Comics launches a new Suicide Squad with an explosive Volume 1. Dive into the dark and ruthless world of the latest Suicide Squad series from DC Comics as they embark on a mission filled with chaos and betrayal.

A promising start for an exciting series

The first volume of Kicked in the Teeth is a promising start for the series, hinting at more exciting and action-packed adventures to come.

A dark and violent tale that will leave no one unscathed

Suicide Squad: Kicked in the Teeth is the first volume of the new DC Comics series dedicated to the team of super-villains. Written by Robbie Thompson and Dennis Hopeless, this new series offers a dark and violent tale that will leave no one unscathed.

The story unfolds in a Gotham City ravaged by gang warfare. Amanda Waller, the director of A.R.G.U.S., decides to form a new Suicide Squad, composed of the most dangerous villains in the city.

A team of villains with troubled motivations

The Suicide Squad is a team of villains with troubled motivations. They are all willing to do anything to survive, and they won't hesitate to betray each other to achieve their goals.

The group is tasked with retrieving a powerful artifact that could endanger Gotham City.

But the members of the Suicide Squad have their own plans, and they won't hesitate to betray each other. Kicked in the Teeth is an explosive start for the new Suicide Squad.

Thompson and Hopeless deliver a dark and violent tale, not skimping on action scenes. The characters are well-developed, and the dialogues are impactful.
